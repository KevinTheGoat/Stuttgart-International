Our Heritage

Built on Passion, Driven by Excellence

For over three decades, Stuttgart International has been Fort Lauderdale's destination for discerning Porsche owners who demand nothing less than perfection. What began as a small shop with a singular focus on German engineering has grown into one of South Florida's most respected collision repair facilities.

Our name pays homage to the birthplace of Porsche - Stuttgart, Germany - and reflects our commitment to the same precision and craftsmanship that defines every vehicle bearing the Stuttgart crest. We don't just repair cars; we restore the driving experience that makes owning a Porsche special.

As a Porsche Approved Collision Center, we've invested in the training, equipment, and facilities required to meet the manufacturer's most stringent standards. Every technician on our team has been factory-certified, and every repair uses genuine OEM parts and approved methods.

"We treat every vehicle as if it were our own, because we understand what it means to be a Porsche enthusiast."
